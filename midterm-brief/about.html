About Flightdeck. And what we do.

At Flightdeck we build sophisticated flight training devices along with all the components that go into them. We help major airlines and thousands of pilots worldwide to excel at their jobs. A common passion for aviation keeps us motivated, and we are immensely proud to contribute towards the highest standards of safety and quality training within the industry.
